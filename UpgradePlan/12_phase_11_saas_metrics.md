# ✅ Phase 11 — Multi-Tenant Intelligence (SaaS Moat)

**Goal:** Deliver value across the entire ecosystem by benchmarking anonymized data.

### 🌐 Cross-Store Insights
AI analyzes aggregated, anonymized trends across multiple organizations.

**Value Delivery:**
- Inform store owners of global trends: *"Stores in your niche are seeing a 30% increase in AOV by bundling Product X with Product Y."*
- Identifying supply chain disruptions before they hit: *"Supply delay detected for Category Z across the network. Consider restocking now."*

### 🛠️ Requirement
Requires a dedicated, schema-isolated **Aggregated Analytics Layer** that strips all PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
