# ✅ Phase 8 — AI Customer Brain (CRM Autopilot)

**Goal:** Automate retention and loyalty without human intervention.

### 🧠 Analysis Metrics
AI constantly monitors:
- **Order Frequency**: Is the customer buying less often?
- **Recency/Monetary Value**: When was the last purchase and how much?
- **Segment Transitions**: Is a "High Value" customer moving to "Churn Risk"?

### 🤖 Decision Table
| Condition | AI Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Churn Risk Detected** | Automatically send a "We Miss You" SMS with a 15% discount. |
| **High Value Milestone** | Grant a "VIP" loyalty reward or exclusive early access. |
| **Discount Sensitivity** | Send targeted coupons only to users who wait for sales. |

Execution is handled via the **AI Action Gateway** connecting to `crm.service`.
