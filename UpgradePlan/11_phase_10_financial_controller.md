# ✅ Phase 10 — AI Financial Controller

**Goal:** Regular financial audits and strategic revenue insights.

### 📅 Weekly Job
**Process:**
- AI analyzes Revenue trends, Margin drops, and Supplier cost fluctuations.
- AI detects anomalies (e.g., *"Shipping costs increased by 20% compared to average"*).

### 📄 Output
AI generates a **Weekly Financial Digest** visible in the dashboard:
- "Revenue vs. Target" progress.
- "Leaked Revenue" detection (carts, returns).
- Cost optimization suggestions (e.g., *"Switching Category X to Supplier B could save 5% monthly"*).
