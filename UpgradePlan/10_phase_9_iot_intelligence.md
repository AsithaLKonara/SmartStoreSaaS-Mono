# ✅ Phase 9 — IoT Incident Intelligence

**Goal:** Prevent physical stock loss through real-time AI triage.

### 🚨 Alert Flow
1. **Sensor Trigger**: Fridge temperature crosses threshold OR motion detected at odd hours.
2. **AI Triage**: Telemetry data is sent to the AI Orchestrator.
3. **Evaluation**: AI determines the severity and potential financial loss (e.g., *"Fridge #2 failure could cost $2,000 in spoiled inventory"*).
4. **Action**: AI sends an emergency notification to the store manager and creates a formal incident log.

### 🛠️ Automation
AI can automatically:
- Escalate high-severity issues to senior management.
- Log events for insurance/compliance purposes.
